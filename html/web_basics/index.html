James Bond's Best Flicks

James Bond has gone through loads of adventures. Those include 23 movies, (25 if you include movies produced by other studio's!), ranging in setting from space stations to underwater fortress' to a New Orleans rife with voodoo magic.

Bond has also faced his fair share of evil supervillans! Some examples include:

  • Jaws
  • Goldfinger
  • The Man with the Golden Gun!

Critics generally rate James Bond films in the following order:

  1. Goldfinger
  2. Dr. No
  3. From Russia with Love

Although, recent Daniel Craig flicks Skyfall and Casino Royale have also begun to crack many folks' lists of top Bond flicks! In fact, this Rotten Tomatoes editorial even has Skyfall and Casino Royale as #1 and #2, respectively! HERESY! While Skyfall is my personal #2, nothing can ever supllant Goldfinger as the best Bond flick of all time.

Alex's top 5 Bond flick's:

  1. Goldfinger
  2. Skyfall
  3. Goldeneye
  4. The Spy Who Loved Me
  5. From Russia With Love

Hope you enjoyed this brief introduction to Bond's best flicks! Watch a few on your own, and see if you come up with a different list!
