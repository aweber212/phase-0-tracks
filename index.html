The New York Derby

A Brief Overview

  • Prior to 2015, there was only one MLS team in New York City, the New York Red Bulls.
  • MLS, ever hungering for expansion, agreed to a proposal for an expansion team, mostly funded by Premier League Manchester City.
  • This new team, NYCFC, finally entered the league in 2015
  • 2015 was the first year of the so called "New York Derby", with three head to head match ups.
    1. 5/10/15. Red Bulls win 2-1.
    2. 6/28/15. Red Bulls win 3-1.
    3. 8/9/15. Red Bulls win 2-0.
  • 2016 has had just one matchup so far, with the Red Bulls clobbering NYCFC 7-0 in the most lopsided rivalry game in MLS history.
